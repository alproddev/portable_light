What Is the Portable Light Project?

Portable Light Project is working to bring clean energy and light to the more than 2 billion people living without electricity in the developing world. Using “trickle up” distribution, the world’s poorest people can now benefit from advanced solar nano-technology and accelerate a global movement towards clean energy use worldwide.

The Portable Light mat can also charge cell phones — allowing communities off the traditional telephone grid access to outside medical aid and supplies. Learn More
